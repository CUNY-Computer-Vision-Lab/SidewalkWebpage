No Sidewalk

A No Sidewalk label should be placed if there is a missing sidewalk where there should be exist one.

Where and how should I place a No Sidewalk label?

You should place one label where the sidewalk ends and another label where the sidewalk begins again. If an entire block or more has no sidewalk, then you should place a label at the beginning and end of each street. If two intersecting streets both have no sidewalk, then place a single label on the corner.

You should not place No Sidewalk labels in narrow side streets or alleys.

What should I do if I reach a 3-way intersection that has no sidewalk?

You should place a no sidewalk label in the center of the opposite street where a sidewalk would be.

Do I place a No Sidewalk label on a median?

You should not place No Sidewalk labels on a median that separates the two directions of traffic, unless the median has a sidewalk that abruptly ends.
